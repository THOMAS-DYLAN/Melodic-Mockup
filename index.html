Our Story

Try your best — your dream will come true.

Before this workshop existed, our team were strangers with day jobs scattered across engineering, teaching, and cooking. When the pandemic took that work away, the one thing we all shared was a love of art. An afternoon spent watching a video on pouring epoxy resin into keycaps turned into a shared obsession — and that obsession became Melodic Artisan.

Every keycap and lamp is still hand-poured and hand-painted in our Hải Dương workshop. No two pieces come out quite the same, which is exactly the point.
